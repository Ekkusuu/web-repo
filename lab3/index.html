Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you don't find what you're looking for, reach out through our contact form.

What is Endfield Factory Planner? + −

It's a free web-based tool that calculates optimal factory layouts for Arknights: Endfield. You select a target item, specify your desired production rate, and it shows you the complete recipe tree, required buildings, and how to connect them.

How accurate are the calculations? + −

All recipes and production times are sourced directly from the game data. The calculator computes exact ratios to ensure 100% efficiency with zero idle time. Building counts are rounded up to give you the minimum whole buildings needed.

Is this similar to Factorio's production planner? + −

Yes! Arknights: Endfield shares many factory mechanics with Factorio. If you've used tools like Factorio Calculator or Kirk McDonald's planner, you'll feel right at home. We've adapted the concept specifically for Endfield's recipe chains.

What buildings are supported? + −

We support all 7 production building types: Greenhouse, Grinder, Smelter, Workshop, Mixer, Assembler, and Chemical Lab. Each building has its own production speed and recipe set built into our database.

Will you update recipes for new game patches? + −

Absolutely. Our game data specialist monitors every patch and beta update. Recipe changes are typically reflected in our database within 24 hours of a new game version release.

Can I use this on mobile? + −

Yes! The entire site is fully responsive. The calculator, recipe trees, and connection schemas all adapt to smaller screens so you can plan on the go.

Is there a way to save my calculations? + −

The free tier shows calculations in real-time but doesn't persist them. With the Pro plan, you can save and load layouts, and even export your schemas as PNG or SVG images.
