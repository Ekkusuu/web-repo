Perlica is the Supervisor of Endfield Industries, a position she created. In this role, she oversees all company operations and serves as the official spokesperson. She is also a leading expert of Techno-Protocol and the development and application of Protocol-Originium technology, which makes her indispensible to the overall operations of the OMV Dijiang. Her past contributions include spearheading key research that enabled the adaptive use of this technology and assisting in the refinement of the Automated Industry Complex (AIC).

Perlica is characterized by her calm and decisive leadership during crises. While the Endministrator was in hibernation, she assumed command of the Crisis Team, leading its personnel against significant threats. She travels extensively throughout the Civilization Band to maintain order and ensure the operational continuity of Endfield Industries.
